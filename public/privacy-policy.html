Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Sangria Cricket Club ("SCC", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Sangria Cricket Club mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Name - to identify you as a club member
  • Phone number - for match-day communication and WhatsApp group coordination
  • Email address - for account identification and optional notifications
  • Date of birth - to celebrate member birthdays within the club
  • Profile photo - optional, for member identification within the App
  • Match availability responses - when you respond to squad polls
  • Payment information - transaction records for club fund deposits and match fees (processed securely via Razorpay; we do not store card or bank details)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for club management purposes:

  • Managing club membership and member profiles
  • Tracking match participation and squad availability
  • Managing club finances (member balances, match fees, expenses)
  • Communicating match schedules and club announcements
  • Displaying cricket performance statistics and leaderboards
  • Processing online payments through Razorpay

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (powered by PostgreSQL), a cloud database hosted in secure data centers. Profile photos and match photos are stored in Supabase Storage. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only accessible to:

  • Club administrators (for managing the club)
  • Other club members (name, profile photo, and cricket stats are visible within the App)
  • Razorpay (only during payment processing, governed by their own privacy policy)

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you are a member of Sangria Cricket Club. If you leave the club or request data deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw from the App at any time

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
