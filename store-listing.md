## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
Sangria Cricket Club (SCC) is the all-in-one app for managing your cricket club. Built by players, for players — track every match, manage member finances, poll squad availability, and stay on top of the season.

KEY FEATURES:

MATCH MANAGEMENT
Track all your matches — external and internal (Dhurandars vs Bazigars). Record results, scores, Man of the Match, and upload team photos. View your full match history with win/loss records.

SQUAD AVAILABILITY POLLS
Before every match, create a poll and share it with the team via WhatsApp. Members mark themselves as Available, Maybe, or Unavailable. See responses in real-time and plan your playing XI.

MEMBER PROFILES
Maintain a roster of all club members with profiles, photos, and contact info. See who's active, who's played recently, and birthday reminders for the team.

FINANCIAL TRACKING
Keep club finances transparent. Track member deposits, match fees, expenses, and refunds. See individual balances, monthly reports, and who has low balance. Online payments supported via Razorpay.

SEASON LEADERBOARD
See who's leading the season — top batsman, top bowler, best fielder, and the overall Season MVP. Performance stats updated after every match.

AI INSIGHTS
Get AI-powered analysis of your team's performance, strengths, weaknesses, and suggestions for the next match. Chat with the AI coach for personalised insights.

TOURNAMENT TRACKER
Track tournaments your club participates in. Link tournament matches, see group stages, knockout rounds, and overall results.

CALENDAR VIEW
See all upcoming and past matches on a clean month-by-month calendar. Never miss a match day.

MATCH DAY TOOLS
Generate squad graphics, share match day messages on WhatsApp, and manage everything you need on game day.

DARK MODE
Full dark mode support for comfortable use at any time.

Built with love for the cricket community. Whether you're a weekend warrior club or a serious amateur team, SCC keeps your club organised and your players informed.
